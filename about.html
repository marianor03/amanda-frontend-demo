How it works

About Amanda

Amanda is a private AI companion designed to help you feel heard. She listens without judgement and is available whenever you need her.

Private by design

Your conversations are stored securely and never shared. You are always in control of what you share.

Always available

Amanda is here 24/7 whenever you need someone to talk to — no appointments, no waiting rooms.

Judgement-free

Share anything on your mind freely. Amanda listens with empathy and without criticism.

What Amanda can help with

Amanda is designed to support people navigating relationship challenges, emotional stress, and everyday life difficulties. She can help you process your feelings, explore your thoughts, and feel less alone.

She is not a replacement for professional mental health care, but a compassionate starting point — a space where you can be honest without fear of judgement.

Who built Amanda?

Amanda is a final-year Computer Science project built by a team of four students passionate about using technology to support emotional wellbeing. She combines modern AI with a thoughtful, human-centred design.
